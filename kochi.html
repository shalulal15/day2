Eranakulam

Ernakulam bordering the district of Thrissur in the north, Idukki in the East, Alapuzha and Kottayam in the south, Lakshadweep Sea in the west is an amalgam of a hoary heritage and global growth of industry and commerce.

Explore the magnificent attractions and experience the vibrant culture of this city.

For the outside world, Ernakulam is part of Kochi referred to in the history of Kerala as the Queen of Arabian Sea.

The densely populated Ernakulam District embodies the achievements of the Kerala State in literacy, industry, trade and commerce.
