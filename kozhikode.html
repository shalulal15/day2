Kozhikode

The Kozhikode Municipal Corporation has a corporation limit population of 609,224[6] and a metropolitan population of more than 2 million, making Kozhikode metropolitan area the second-largest in Kerala and the 19th largest in India.

Kozhikode is classified as a Tier 2 city by the Government of India.[8] NIT Calicut, NIEIT and IIM Kozhikode are institutions of national importance located in the district.

In antiquity and the medieval period, Kozhikode was dubbed the City of Spices for its role as the major trading point for Indian spices
